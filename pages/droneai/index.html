Flying AI

A Flying AI has a very complex movement logic. Instead of using traditional A* for Path-finding on a plane (Navmesh), it uses directional vectors which are influenced by the objects around it to avoid any collisions.
Also, it doesnot use simple translation, instead it uses a rigidbody simulation to smoothout the overall movement and add actual flying force on the drone gameobject itself.

The Setup


The movement system uses a complex array of "Sensors" which feeds important information to perception component, which decides the influence vectors for the force applied on the drone.

Movement

The movement uses a constant rigidbody force in the direction vector determined by taking in the factors for Sensors data. When fed in to perception, results in to obstacle avoidance while maintaining its flying nature.



Demo

The whole module can be used to in any complex scenes with many obstacles. Interestingly, it is also able to find a "Escape window" and it will try to fly from that if its size is appropriate enough.
